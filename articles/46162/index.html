iQue Grill - grill with computer and built-in stereo system



Attempting to fry meat in deathly silence is a bad dream for any cook. Especially if you need to cook a lot. That is why most housewives put on the kitchen TV, or at worst, the radio.

The iQue Blue Ember 650 is an ultramodern grill machine whose main task is to turn cooking into a party. It is intended, of course, not for city apartments, but for cottages, and includes almost everything you need for a good party.
')
Culinary functionality of the machine includes two cooking surfaces, two grills with a hood, and an infrared burner for cooking on a spit. At the same time the hood allows to use the device indoors. All this joy weighs 82 kilograms.

The on-board computer has a touch screen and can be programmed for almost automatic cooking. Depending on such factors as the type of meat (beef, pork, chicken), grilled steers, etc. the hostess chooses the desired mode, and then it remains only to wait for the moment when the dish is ready.



And yes ... music. As an option, a refrigerator and a stereo can be built into the grill instead of the downstairs. The latter, in fact, is the main generator of the wow effect, which is expected in iQue, according to one of the directors of the manufacturer, Peter Perley, given in the product presentation.
