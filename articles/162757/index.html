THQ and nVidia give out Metro 2033 for free



The company THQ has prepared a gift for gamers for the new year - free keys for the Windows version of the grim action movie Metro 2033 .

All you need is to visit a special Metro page on Facebook, like a post and agree to receive a newsletter about the game. The offer is valid until December 16, 2012 ( today is the last day ).

PS: THQ is very soon to pay a debt of $ 50 million. Humble Bundle , by the way, brought the publisher $ 4.5 million. Compared to the amount of debt, of course, quite a bit. THQ is currently negotiating with an undisclosed investor who can save the company from bankruptcy.
