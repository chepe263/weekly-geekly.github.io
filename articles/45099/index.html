Egg-shaped mobile



Obviously, the time development of mobile phone form factors has slowly stalled. Traditional monoblock bricks and their iphone without the keyboard, clamshell, sliders ... from the mass options, did not seem to miss anything. Everything else is either highly specialized or simply uncomfortable. However, the search continues. In the illustrations you see another option. This time from the Czech designer Roman Tubl. It is assumed that this will be a phone for children and the elderly. Convenient, easy to use, with touch screen. Of the features, we note the USB-out, mp3-player and the ability to change the color of the backlight. We'll see, as they say, what the manufacturers will say, especially since the same author has quite a few traditional models in stock.
