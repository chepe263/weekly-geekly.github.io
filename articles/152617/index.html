G8 Summit - everyone will be there

On October 26, sales of new tablets, laptops, tablet computers and notebooks on Windows 8 will begin worldwide.
And on October 23, we gather in Moscow the G8 Summit - Windows 8 Summit , in order to discuss this significant event for the industry and each of the billion users in the world together with the leaders of the Russian application market.

Windows 8 Summit is an event that is interesting both for developers who create applications and for businessmen who make money on them. And in the world of mobile applications, these roles are often combined.

Today we have updated the conference program , and additions will be made to it regularly until the event itself.
Nevertheless, today we can say that all the major players in the Russian market will be there.
')
These are leading device manufacturers, and the largest portals (Yandex, Mail.Ru Group), and design studios (UsabilityLab, UIDesign), and custom developers (i-Free, Galssoft, e-Legion, ActisWunderman). We also invite leading Russian game developers, experts in monetization and promotion of applications.

We decided to split the program into 2 parts. The first is business discussions and discussions with market experts. The second is technological immersion in the development, testing and publication of applications.
I also want to remind you that Windows Store members ( are you not there yet ?) Receive a ticket to the Summit for free.

3 days before the launch of Windows 8, we will discuss who and how much will earn on it. Join now!
