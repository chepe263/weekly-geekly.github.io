GTA fans shot a GTA-like video using a drone



Grand Theft Auto series has long been a super-popular series. Millions of players do mafia tasks every day, enjoying the gameplay. Now, with the release of the fifth part, the online world has appeared in the toy, GTA online. The graphics here are more and more perfect, and the gameplay is more and more realistic.

However, there are still fans of the previous versions of the game, which were developed in 2D. For fans of the previous parts of the day, the video was shot by the same fans.
