Deploy4Me reduces prices and fixes them in rubles



After receiving a lot of positive reviews after the previous article , we decided to consolidate our success and meet the users. Therefore, Deploy4Me announces price cuts for all server software installations in the AWS cloud and in the clouds that are already on the way.

For those who have just joined, let me remind you that Deploy4Me is a service for deploying business software to the cloud. Without coding and without short circuit on one vendor. We started recently, but with Hope we are looking to the future.

The first users from Habr who have stepped onto the site have greatly expanded the boundaries of our geography and knowledge about the world. Therefore, we decided to keep up and expand supported currencies and countries. Today we have added support for Russian rubles, Czech crowns, Indian rupees and British pounds.

Especially for users from Russia (and those who prefer to pay in rubles), we fixed the ruble prices . We hope that this will support the interest in the service and will be a small gift for the upcoming New Year.
