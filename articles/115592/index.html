Video review reader Highscreen Alex with two screens

Hello, book lovers,

Recently, we talked about the compact smartphone Highscreen Cosmo . Today we will talk about another device available in Russia under the brand Highscreen. This is a two-screen reader Highscreen Alex based on Android . Its upper screen is a classic e-paper, the lower one is a color LCD display for control and navigation.


')
The Android interface on the device is changed. The main screen is a set of vertical buttons that can be customized to your liking. FBreader acts as a reading application. The program is quite suitable, with great potential and also adapted to the screen of the device.

Pleases the presence of the button "Synchronization" , which makes identical content on both screens. The reader is equipped with a 624 MHz processor, 2 GB of internal memory and a microSD card with 2 GB, as well as a WiFi module. The latter allows you to download books directly from the Internet, install applications (specially adapted for the device) and even use the browser.

The software also includes a music player (by the way, good headphones are included), news readers for print media (MK and Sovetsky Sport, others can be downloaded), two book catalogs (AyMobilko and LitRes), a dictionary and an application for viewing HTML, PDF and TXT files.

In addition, Highscreen Alex has good dimensions. The reader is very light, thin and has a good price of 9 990 rubles, which is important.
