Screenshot Editor at Yandex.Disk

Today, Yandex.Disk for Windows and OS X has a screenshot editor . What many previously organized with symlinks and scripts now works out of the box. Not to mention a convenient and specially designed for working with screenshots editor.



The keyboard shortcuts for taking screenshots remain standard - Prnt Scrn on Windows and Cmd + Shift + 4 on OS X. You will immediately see a concise editor with tools: a set of arrows and shapes of different colors, a marker and a blur tool. Highlighting important, you can in one click to save your screenshot on your computer or get a short link to it.

')
You no longer need to install additional programs and think where to download the image. Now it is not difficult to make step-by-step instructions for parents or send a friend a screenshot of a funny dialogue in the messenger. It will take you just a few tens of seconds. All screenshots are saved on the Disk, in the Screenshots folder.

An updated version of the Yandex.Disk program with a built-in editor can be downloaded at disk.yandex.ru/screenshot
