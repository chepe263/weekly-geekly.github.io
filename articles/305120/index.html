Sailfish OS - Summer School at Innopolis University for mobile developers and Linux enthusiasts



From July 27 to July 30, Innopolis University will host the first summer school dedicated to the Sailfish OS platform. Open Mobile Platform invites students, graduate students, application developers and enthusiasts who value Linux-based projects. School participants expect introductory lectures, meeting with representatives of the developer community, technical master classes, a programming contest, an entertainment program, and informal communication.
