Opera representatives in universities

From today, Opera Software has launched a program to introduce Opera and web standards to universities all over the world. Anyone can become a volunteer for the Opera Campus Crew team and become part of the team of representatives of the Opera in schools and universities in different countries.
Read more at www.opera.com/education/tours

In the near future, Opera is touring France, China and the UK. Living nearby can get to the seminars organized by the Opera.
Seminar Schedules: www.opera.com/education/tours
Community meetings: www.choose.opera.com
