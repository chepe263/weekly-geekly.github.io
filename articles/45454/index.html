Vahu.Ru - Portal about products and companies

Rate a new startup - beta.vahu.ru

Our goal is to make a service where a person will be able to find information he is interested in about a particular product or service (technical specifications, usage tips, consumer reviews, etc.), information about companies.

The consumer encyclopedia section is intended for those interested in goods or services. Here you can both read the reviews and opinions of other users, and post your own articles. If the information of interest was not found, you can "leave a request" on the relevant topic.
')


The encyclopedia is divided into several sections with subsections. When switching sections displays a list of recent articles and subsections.

Directory of companies and enterprises. With sections and subsections. Almost as usual.
Only each company, in addition to the standard set of attributes, can have departments that can be called and addresses that can be reached. For a company, its region is indicated, and if the company has a representative office in the region of the user, then it is marked with a corresponding postscript.

If one author is hard to fill out all the information about the company, he can take an assistant for himself.

The next stage, it is planned to make a national rating of companies. For each company from the catalog you can leave a review and assessment. You can even compare companies of the same specialization with each other. Responding to reviews on behalf of the company will be able to users tied to the relevant company.

It is interesting to know your opinion about the site as a whole. About all sorts of flaws, flaws, errors.

I would also like to know your opinion about one feature: when placing an article, registering a company, and for any other action that requires user identification, the user can register or log in in the same form that he fills out.

About the fact that the idea is not new, I ask you not to shower rotten tomatoes. This site is part of a big future project. But since the entire project is not ready yet, and this site can already live independently, we decided to post it earlier.
