About company.
Interregional TransitTelecom (MTT) is one of the largest telecom operators, which according to the CNews portal rating, ranks sixth in terms of revenue among Russian telecommunication companies. Today, MTT provides IP-telephony services, intelligent network services (ISS), numbering 8-800, mobile communications (MVNO), traditional MG / ILD communications for individuals and corporate clients, as well as traffic transit for other telecom operators. In 1993, the company was created as an operator for an operator, and at the moment it effectively provides for interaction of more than 300 networks of cellular operators and more than 100 networks of fixed-line operators in Russia among themselves and with most foreign operators.
