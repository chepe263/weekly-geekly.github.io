The introduction of PR: a scandal in the American blogosphere

The authors of the blog Wal-Marting Across America , spouses Laura and Jim, went on a journey across America - from Las Vegas to Georgia. At the same time, they stayed for the night exclusively in the parking lots of the Wal-Mart supermarket chain, where parking is even free for sleeping trailers. The virtual journey began on September 27, 2006. It was on this day that the first blog entry was published. Now, when a real scandal has erupted in the blogosphere, all previous entries have been erased from the site.

Laura almost every day published new photos and impressions about her journey, which was thoroughly imbued with optimism. Every employee of the Wal-Mart supermarket, whom they met on their way, from a regular clerk to a nice manager, was absolutely satisfied with his work and eternally grateful to his beloved employer.

This is an important detail, because in fact, as many Americans know, Wal-Mart employees receive low wages (about $ 10 per hour, almost like at McDonalds) and do not treat their employer very well.
