Google announced the launch of the service SearchWiki. With it, users will be able to influence the issuance of search results - so far only in their own account and only in the English version.
The function allows users to change and customize the position of pages in the search results of the service.
With this system, each user with an account on the service can raise their favorite sites up in search results, and also exclude from it those pages that he does not like.
The launch of SearchWiki was the first time that a popular search engine allowed users to personalize search results. According to company representatives, this innovation should make the search more personal and diverse.
