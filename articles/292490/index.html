Yandex is going to increase the number of mobile developers

Yandex is planning to allocate Yandex.Browser and mobile technologies into an autonomous unit, which will be headed by Dmitry Polishchuk. Earlier, Polishchuk led the development of the company's mobile services, Vedomosti reports. The analytical division AppMetrica also entered the new division. Mobile applications for most of the company's services (such as Taxi, Maps, Mail, and others) will be developed by the staff of the relevant Yandex departments. The mobile division will specialize in creating new services and increasing the expertise of the company in the mobile Internet.

As part of this project, the company intends to recruit about 100 new specialists from Moscow, St. Petersburg and Novosibirsk. Most new employees will be busy in the mobile direction. Yandex now needs developers, designers, project managers, product specialists and testers, according to search engine representative Ekaterina Karnaukhova. Now 150 people work at Yandex Browser, and the company has a total of 5,600 employees.
