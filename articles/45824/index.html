10,000 Applications

In addition, on the page you can find other interesting information. Approximately 24% of applications are free, 35% cost $ .99. The average cost of the application is $ 3.12, including free. About 34% of applications are games and entertainment, 49 applications are weather, despite the fact that you can learn about the weather from the preinstalled iPhone program.
