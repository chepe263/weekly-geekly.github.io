New LinkedIn Search Platform

Since yesterday, a new search platform has been introduced on LinkedIn , which makes it much easier to find the right people. An overview of the new functionality can be viewed in a demo video , and in short the main innovations are reduced to several things:

1) more criteria for accurate search;
2) ranking the results according to the relevance algorithm, which is personalized especially for you;
3) the ability to save search results (up to three) and receive email notifications in the event of the appearance of a new candidate that meets the search criteria;
4) spell checker for names and surnames;
5) auto-complete the search line by name and surname.
