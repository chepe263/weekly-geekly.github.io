Two Startup Weekends in a row

Three weeks before Startup Weekend in Moscow, we received so many applications for participation that we decided (yes, that there we had to!) To hold two Startup Weekends in a row, and not one.

Startup Weekend February 17-19. Getting started on the project.


First, there will be Startup Weekend for already formed teams that can work out their idea with experts, develop a work plan and get initial investments for it.
To participate, along with the description of the idea, you will need to send an idea to team@glavstart.ru as well.
We toughened the selection specifically to ensure that teams that received investments based on the results of the event were able to immediately get to work, and not waste time searching for co-founders.

Startup Weekend February 25-26. Putting the team.


The second Weekend will be held specifically for the authors of ideas who need a team and investment, and for specialists who would like to take part in creating a web project and become a co-founder of a promising business.
If you have an idea for a web project, tell us about it in a free-form letter to idea@glavstart.ru
If you are a good specialist and want to become a co-founder of a web project, write to us about yourself and your experience at star@glavstart.ru (Programmers, marketers, interface workers and sales people are always needed, but about the rarer professions that our chosen projects will need let you know more to the point)
