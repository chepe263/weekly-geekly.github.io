Youtube 3D video is better

Since yesterday, Youtube can watch 3D video in a new mode, that is, with hardware support for Nvidia 3D Vision (Nvidia GeForce with Driver Release 275 or newer). True, this feature for WebM video format is supported only in Firefox 4 browser.

Over the past couple of years on the video hosting posted a few thousand 3D videos, they can be found on the tag yt3d . To view, you must also select the 3D mode in the Youtube settings.

Mozilla developers hope that other browsers will follow suit and also add support for 3D HTML5 video in good quality.
