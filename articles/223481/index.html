AT & T flooded New York charging stations for smartphones

The weakest point of smartphones, in my opinion, is their attachment to the charger. If simple phones can work for several days, and Philips Xenium is a month in general, then the flagships from HTC, Samsung, and even Apple, usually withstand only one smartphone day.

AT & T, one of the largest US telecommunications companies, in order to make the use of smartphones more convenient, has placed solar charging stations all over New York.


')
In New York, the company placed 45 such stations, which is two times more than in 2013, because the initiative was first tested last year. Now uses a new version of the stations, which is less affected by weather conditions.

The service is absolutely free - anyone can charge a smartphone or tablet, even if it is not an AT & T customer. And the stations operate even at night, thanks to batteries that charge during the day from the sun.

The project is launched until September, so if you find yourself in New York in the summer, remember about saving charging stations.
