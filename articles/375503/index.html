Ilon Mask has confirmed plans to build a test track Hyperloop

Ilon Musk in his official twitter confirmed that the test track of his new project Hyperloop will be built, and it is likely that this will happen in Texas. The head of SpaceX did not name the specific date or any other details regarding the construction plans.

The Hyperloop project is a system of passenger and cargo transportation through a piping system with evacuated air in special capsules. Musk calls Hyperloop the fifth mode of transport (in addition to water, air, road, rail), believing that the goal of the project should be to connect megalopolises that are about a thousand kilometers apart.

The real appearance of Hyperloop in the form of a usable transport system is possible in ten years, experts say . The cost of construction will be from 7 to 19 billion dollars.
