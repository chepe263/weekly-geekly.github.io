Nostalgia for sega

Japanese comrades from Akiba PC, deciding, it seems, to recall the past, made an amusing modding. With the help of fantasy and such a mother of skilled hands from the old Dreamcast console, the Japanese turned out to be a modern PC.



In the console case, they fit the AMD mini-ITX motherboard with a 780G chipset, an ATI Radeon HD 3200 video card, 4 GB of RAM and a 160-gigabyte 2.5-inch hard drive. Also, the HDMI output was added.


It cost the whole thing about $ 1000. It is clear that modding is not for sale. The guys had fun.
