Rospotrebnadzor proposes blocking unscrupulous online shopping

The other day Rospotrebnadzor appealed to the government with a proposal to block unscrupulous online stores and aggregators. According to a similar scheme, officials act in the event of the suspension of work of ordinary retail outlets and restaurants, Interfax reports. Rospotrebnadzor proposes to use a temporary blocking of the online store at the domain name level when violations are detected.

Representatives of the agency expressed concern that online stores usually find themselves outside the jurisdiction of consumer protection law. The work of aggregators, who do not sell goods at all, but buyers transfer money to aggregators, which then go to the seller, is also not regulated in the legislative field.
