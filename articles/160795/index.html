The Humble THQ Bundle Launched!

This time, the Humble Bundle presents us games from the developer and publisher THQ Inc. Unfortunately, the games are available only for Windows and only through Steam .

List of available games :
  • Darksiders
  • Metro 2033
  • Red Faction: Armageddon
  • Company of Heroes
  • Company of Heroes: Opposing Fronts
  • Company of Heroes: Tales of Valor
  • Saints Row: The Third (for those who pay more than average)

List of available DRM-Free soundtracks:
  • Saints Row: The Third - The Soundtrack (MP3)
  • Darksiders Original Soundtrack - Directors Cut (MP3)
  • Company of Heroes: All Heroes Rise (FLAC and MP3)
  • Company of Heroes: Songs From the Front (FLAC and MP3)

The total cost of games and soundtracks, if you buy them separately, will be $ 190. As always, part of the money received will go to charity organizations (Child's Play Charity, the American Red).
