ZTE introduced its modular smartphone ECO-MOBIUS

At the China International Exhibition, which was held on September 24-28, 2013, ZTE company, known not only in China, but also in the world, presented its version of a modular smartphone called ECO-MOBIUS, which the user can freely assemble or upgrade.


Photos from the exhibition:

This year, the concept phone has received an authoritative award in the field of design Red Dot Award .
The smartphone consists of 4 modules: processor, display, battery and camera. The main module, which consists of a screen, is inserted into the CPU, GPU, RAM, camera, memory card and battery.

All components are mounted in grooves using a magnet. The whole thing is covered with a lid.
