NskConf Developer Conference


Are you a student and do not know what interesting and useful events to visit in the summer?

Are you an IT specialist and you are worried about pressing issues in your professional activity?

Or do you just want to hang out in the circles of the IT-community?

Then head for NskConf! Very soon!

We invite all IT people to the NskConf conference, which will be held on July 9 in Novosibirsk . The organizer is Softline company.

Our goal is to gather students and IT specialists to discuss current issues in the following sections:

  • Project management
  • Development
  • Analytics
