Interview with Boris Kim (QIWI) at the conference # MBLT13

On May 15, the international mobile conference # MBLT13 was held in Moscow. More than 400 participants and 30 speakers from the USA, Japan, Great Britain, Singapore, the Netherlands, Italy, Germany and Russia gathered in the Digital October center. Representatives of companies such as Foursquare, TechCrunch, Google, IBM, VentureBeat, Singularity University, TripAdvisor and many others spoke.



The first video we would like to show is an interview with Boris Kim (member of the Board of Directors of the QIWI Group). What is Qiwi planning to do after the IPO? How did Qiwi create user friendly interfaces for users? PayPal in Russia, the attitude to the Yandex.Money competitor and many other issues were discussed with Boris by the former chairman of the board of Tinkoff Bank, and now Georgy Chesakov, the creator of his own financial startup.
