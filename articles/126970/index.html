Sharp has released the world's first tablet with NFC chip



The other day the announcement of a new Android-tablet, released by Sharp. Tablet as a tablet, quite nice, but not particularly different from the mass of other similar devices. Its only difference is the NFC chip, which allows you to make quick payments in stores, restaurants and cafes (well, vending machines), which have the appropriate equipment. The tablet model was named RW-T107, and it looks like this is really the first tablet in the world with a similar chip.

By the way, first of all the tablet RW-T107 will arrive first in the stores of Japan, in the amount of 5 thousand pieces. It is in this country that NFC technology has so far received the most widespread.
