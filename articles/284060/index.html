Wargaming closes Kharkiv office

Network publication AIN announces the closure of the Kharkov office development company Wargaming.net.
Recall: the company supports the popular multiplayer game World Of Tanks.

All 50 employees were offered a move to Kiev or Minsk.

Representatives of Wargaming explained to AIN that management is changing the structure of the web department to achieve "maximum development efficiency, integration and optimization of work."

Prior to this, there was a message from Kharkov about the closure of the offices of Persha Studia. In 2011, Persha and Wargaming signed an exclusive partnership agreement, after which the studio, in fact, became a division of Wargaming.
