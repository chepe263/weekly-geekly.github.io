Keith Chambers' Wood Watch



American Keith Chambers ( Keith Chambers ) - a watchmaker. For the past five years, the subject of his pride and means of earning is a wooden clock. Each new model is similar to the previous one only in general terms. The master takes at least 100 hours to create it. On average, each watch consists of two hundred parts, of which about three dozen belong to an electric motor, wires and batteries, and Kate makes the remaining 170 with her own hands. Designer's favorite materials are oak, cherry and walnut.
