Vacation in Cyprus and great things

Summer in IT companies is often a period of lull, when employees prefer beach and garden work to prepare new releases. But not in our case: the beginning of summer 2013 turned out to be very fruitful for 2GIS.

Last year we began to conquer further afield, going out in Italy with reference books of Padua and Venice. Then we promised that this was just the beginning, and we can expect the appearance of 2GIS in other countries in the future. We kept a promise: 2GIS came to Cyprus, namely to Limassol.
