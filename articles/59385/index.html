Brandz.Ru: announcement of a new competition - from 11 to 24 May

I am glad to present a new contest for users of the Brandz.Ru advisory network ; this time the flash drives are played out.
In order to take part in the prize draw you need to write an interesting review about a thing or brand (trademark) to your blog on Brandz.Ru.
Winners will determine the popular vote and ... luck.
Voting for reviews is the honorable duty of every Brandz.Ru participant - even if you do not take part in the competition, mark your favorite reviews with your votes, help interesting posts to rise!
It is very simple to vote: it is enough to note the liked reviews "+" or "-".
At the end of the competition, our robot will call 15 reviews, scored the maximum score, and from them will choose the winner at random. We will send him the Transcend JetFlash V30 / 16Gb, which we have already bought and put in the drawer of the table.

And again: The same prize (Transcend JetFlash V33 / 16Gb) will also be received by the one who invited the author of the winning reviewer! In case the winner does not have an invitee, our robot will think and choose the second winner from the remaining 14 lucky ones;)
