Nokia has opened pre-order for new Lumia in Russia: the price of the flagship is 24 990 rubles

Today in the official blog of Nokia there was an entry that announces the beginning of sales of new smartphones of the company, which were presented to the public on September 5 of this year.

The pre-order for the Lumia 920 (in yellow, red, white and black) and 820 (red, white and black body colors) will start from today's date, with no exact sales date yet: only it is reported that it will start in the fourth quarter of 2012 .

As already mentioned in the title, the cost of the flagship model Nokia Lumia 920 will be 24,990 rubles, Nokia Lumia 820 - 19,990 rubles. Also, as a bonus to the flagship, Nokia Purity Stereo Headset headphones are indicated, however with an honest warning that their number is limited.
