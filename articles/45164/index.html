We will pay your fines

Ladies and gentlemen, I present to your attention the attraction of unprecedented generosity: the service of paying fines .

Recently, more and more cameras appear on the streets of Moscow that record violations of traffic rules. Our service was created to help those who got one of these cameras and received a fine by mail. It works very simply: you send us a notice of a fine, and we pay for it. If you have already paid for it yourself - we will refund your money.
