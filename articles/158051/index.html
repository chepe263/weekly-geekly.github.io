The Humble Bundle for Android 4 has started

Started another action from The Humble Bundle .
As usual, you pay as much as you think you need for a set of games - and those who pay more than the average get an extra game. At this time, all the proposed games have a version for Android - which, of course, does not prevent you from putting them on your PC under Windows, OS X or Linux, as well as adding to Steam (if you pay from one dollar).
The list of games currently includes:

  • Crayon Physics Deluxe
  • Eufloria
  • Splice
  • Superbrothers: Sword and Sworcery
  • Waking mars
  • Machinarium (for paid more than average)

I note in particular that the appearance of games as part of the Humble Bundle means their release for OS X and Linux, which is particularly encouraging in the case of Eufloria. System requirements can be found here .
