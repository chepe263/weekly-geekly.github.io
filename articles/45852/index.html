Coming large-scale reform of education and science

Separately, they will be engaged in universities - this rank is planned to be retained only for fifty out of almost four hundred existing ones. The catalyst for the reform, which has been developed by the Ministry of Education and Science for a long time, was the financial crisis. Therefore, a more radical scenario is not excluded, according to which only a third of all existing scientific and educational institutions will survive.
