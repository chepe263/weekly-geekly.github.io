Cheap way to simulate getting a bullet in a person

We in our independent studio "KinoKafe" are now engaged in the production of a new short film action movie . Since there is almost no money, and Napoleonic plans, we try to make everything as simple and cheap as possible, but without compromising quality. In particular, we thought for a long time how to simulate a bullet hitting the human body (without this in an action movie - nowhere). Looked on the Internet, found a wonderful guide .

The bottom line is this. A condom is taken, pre- cooked blood is poured into it, the condom is washed off from the lubricant, tied, and a puck is attached to it with a fishing line attached to it. Then, when the glue dries up, the condom sticks to the person. Then you just need to just pull the line in time - the blood spurts. Very simple, but very effective way, which is important, does not require special skills (in contrast to the pyrotechnics used in large cinema).
