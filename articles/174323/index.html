Video review laptop Lenovo IdeaPad Z400 Touch



Lenovo IdeaPad Z400 Touch is an inexpensive, but productive small-sized notebook. It is equipped with a glossy TN display with a diagonal of 14 inches and a resolution of 1366x768. A feature of the Touch modification is, as it is not difficult to guess, support for touch input.

As for performance, the third-generation Intel Core processor (i3 / i5 / i7) and the Nvidia GeForce GT635M video card are responsible for it. The disk subsystem is represented by conventional hard drives with a rotational speed of 5400 revolutions per minute, with a capacity of 320 GB to 1 TB. RAM is installed from 4 to 8 GB, two slots are available, expandable up to 16 GB.
