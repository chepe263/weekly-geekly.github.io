Confidence returns to venture capitalists

For the first time in the last two years, the Silicon Valley venture financier confidence index has changed in a positive direction , not in a negative one. Perhaps this may be evidence that the bottom of the crisis in the sector of IT start-ups will soon be over.



The venture capitalists confidence index (Silicon Valley Venture Capitalist Confidence Index) is calculated on a five-point scale based on a survey of several dozen representatives of Silicon Valley venture capital funds. They share their feelings, as the venture climate will change in the next 6-18 months. That is, it is a leading indicator. If the feelings of professionals are justified, then the real recovery of venture capital investments will begin to take place in about a year.
