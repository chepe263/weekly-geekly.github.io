Sales reports on the Android Market

Just recently, Google provided an opportunity for developers to receive monthly sales reports on the Android Market. Reports include detailed sales information (before a separate transaction), as well as additional information: device information, sale currency, current conversion rate. In addition, it is possible to fully download reports in a CSV file.



To date, reports are available for March of this year. It also promises that reports will appear for the remaining months to January 2010. In general, reports for the previous month will appear on the tenth day of each month. With all questions and suggestions, it is recommended to contact the Market Help Center .
