VMware and Cisco - VDI-friends forever

Cisco and VMware announced the creation of VDI tools that combine elements of existing solutions from both companies to accelerate the implementation of VDI solutions with customers.
The solution is based on the Cisco Unified Computing System and VMware View 4.5 products and is designed to facilitate the sale of VDI solutions that most effectively solve customer problems.
As both companies say, Cisco Desktop Virtualization Solution with VMware View 4.5 simplifies the solution of issues related to the implementation of virtualization, at the same time improving the perception of VDI users and ensuring productivity and stability.
Cisco and VMware promise to deploy VDI "in minutes" and additional benefits in the form of cheaper components and storage costs.
Other advantages are the preservation of the user's working environment settings regardless of the connection point and the type of device used, as well as the implementation of security policies.
All the details can be found in this document - Solve the Desktop Crisis with Cisco Desktop Virtualization .
