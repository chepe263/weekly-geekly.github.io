Picture gallery of the upcoming netbook ASUS Eee PC S121

The network has information about some of the technical characteristics of the new netbook ASUS Eee PC S121 prepared for release by ASUSTeK Computer, backed up with many high-quality images of this very spectacular product design.



As reported, the debutant is a direct heir of the luxurious and slim model ASUS Eee PC S101 presented in October of the current year, but it can boast the presence of a larger LCD screen with a diagonal of 12.1 inches. In this case, it is noted that the new product will most likely also be equipped with an Intel Atom processor with a clock frequency of 1.6 GHz, a gigabyte of DDR2 RAM, a solid-state drive with a capacity of 16, 32 or 64 GB, and Wi-Fi and Bluetooth modules.

It is expected that the official premiere of this laptop will be held at the January international exhibition CES 2009 in Las Vegas, before the opening of which there is not much time left.
