Deep Zoom is one of the most interesting features of Silverlight 2. I wrote about interesting examples of using this technology for maps and logos the day before .

In the Fashion Viewer section, you can interactively view the fashion show gallery by selecting the appropriate designer. In addition, you can consider the details of the compositions - as outfits, and the models themselves. As usual, this is done with the mouse wheel.
