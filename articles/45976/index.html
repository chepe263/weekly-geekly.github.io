Student startups will receive their award

The Higher School of Economics, together with the British Higher School of Design, has established a new award designed to encourage entrepreneurial initiatives among students. On December 12, the first Student Startup of the Year award ceremony will take place.



Winners will be determined in four nominations:
')
1. "Student startup of the year"
2. "Business person of the year"
3. "The most effective solution"
4. "Design solution"

The organizers want to encourage beautiful and effective entrepreneurial solutions and celebrate a creative approach to building a new business.

If you are not a student, but you have your own project and you are less than 30 years old, you can also be nominated for a prize.

Among the jury members:

1. Dmitry Molchanov, Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Domostroy company,
2. Leonid Feigin, creative director of Direct Design Studio,
3. Konstantin Bocharsky, a journalist and creator of the Territory of Ideas project.
