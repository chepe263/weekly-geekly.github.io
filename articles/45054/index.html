USB flash drive with eSATA

The company Kanguru Solutions has released an unusual flash drive , which, in addition to the USB interface, is also equipped with eSATA.


The idea is simple: since eSATA is much faster than USB, then in a PC, they are equipped with e-Flash (this is how Kanguru called a novelty) should be connected via this interface. If eSATA in the computer is not observed, then USB 2.0 is not so bad.

The data transfer rate of e-Flash via USB is 30 MB / s (read) and 20 MB / s (write). If you use the eSATA interface, the speed reaches 75 Mb / s and 25 Mb / s, respectively, for reading and writing.

The e-Flash cable comes with an eSATA + Power cable.

In the assortment of Kanguru Solutions, while two models of flash drives with eSATA. 16 and 32 GB. They cost $ 85 and $ 120. In January 2009, the company plans to release a 64-gigabyte e-Flash.
