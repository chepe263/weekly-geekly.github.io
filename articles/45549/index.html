ByCamp 2008



The first barcamp conference in Belarus - ByCamp 2008 will be held in Minsk on December 20-21. The themes of the event will be new media, blogging, podcasting, social networks, Internet security and startups - companies starting their businesses.

The conference is unique for Belarus, as it is designed not only for professionals in the IT sphere, but also for ordinary users.

On the first day of the conference, presentations will be held in parallel in 4 classrooms of the Institute of Journalism of the BSU. On the second day, the participants will work in thematic groups, one of which will visit the house-museum of Felix Yanushkevich in Rakov, the other - the Belarusian State Museum of Folk Architecture and Life, the rest will gather in Minsk cafes.

Unique event and its format. Barcamp is a way of holding conferences, in the framework of which participants maximally join the process of organizing an event. They not only help with the technical organization, announce the event in blogs, but also independently create a schedule - everyone can write the topic of his speech, presentations, discussions in an empty table on the morning of the first day of the conference. The conference takes place in an informal atmosphere that stimulates discussion, exchange of ideas and networking.

The arrival of foreign guests from Ukraine, Russia and other neighboring countries is expected. Yandex and Bigmir have already announced their participation.

The partners of the ByCamp 2008 conference are the Institute of Journalism of the Belarusian State University, Atlant Telecom, MTS, and Bigmir-Internet.

Participation in ByCamp 2008 is free. You only need to register on the site bycamp.org and decide on the form of participation. Remember: there are no tourists on BikeCamp!
