JQuery Animated Sprites - Spritely

Spritely - is a jQuery plugin for creating dynamic images and background animation.



With JavaScript capabilities, the animation can be controlled by mouse movement or clicks.
Animated elements can fly on the page without affecting any other element of the page (it may be ideal for banners) and the page is still accessible.
Works where Flash is not supported, even IE6 support is enabled.

PS As an example, on the author's page you can observe a flock of 4 eyed birds - mutants who hunt for clicks of the mouse.
